About Me

Entrepreneur & Engineer

I'm Elon Musk, a technology entrepreneur, investor, and engineer. Born in South Africa, I developed a passion for technology at a young age. I founded X.com, which later became PayPal, and went on to create SpaceX, Tesla, Neuralink, and The Boring Company.

My mission is to make humanity interplanetary, accelerate the transition to sustainable energy, and connect the human brain with artificial intelligence.
